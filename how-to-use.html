Dipole Mode

Simulation Controls

  • Add Dipole: Click Add Dipole to create a new magnet..
  • Move: Grab any magnet and drag it to move it where you want.
  • Rotate: Drag the small purple handle that circles the magnet to rotate it and change its direction.
  • Strength: Use the Strength toggle (Low / Medium / High) to adjust how strong the magnet is.

Wire Mode

Current & Flow

  • Add Wire (+): Spawns a wire with current flowing out of the screen (Green).
  • Add Wire (-): Spawns a wire with current flowing into the screen (Orange).
  • Interaction: Drag wires to observe how their circular magnetic fields interact.
